Step One: Configure your Dropbox!

First, Dropbox doesn't allow just anyone connect. So, you have to tell Dropbox that you are working on an app/site that needs to use Dropbox.

So..

  1. Go here, to the Dropbox Apps page.
  2. Hit the button to create a new App.
  3. Though you can change it later, what you type in the 'name' field will be the folder in Dropbox to look at for your site.
  4. Put this into the description field: 
    Dropbox-Based PHP Site; http://

Step Two: Configure your Site!

OK! So, you've set up the app in Dropbox!

As a note, any spaces in your name help this site figure out your 'first' and 'last' name.
